History & Resources

Since its inception Substation Engineering Services has steadily expanded its operations, whilst developing expertise across a range of Electrical, Construction and Engineering projects. provision on high quality finishing works those meet the client highest expectation. The organization guards, its reputation by exercising tight project financial control and excellent site management in times of difficult situation.

We provide comprehensive specialist services to industry and construction for Design, Installation, Commissioning and Maintenance of transmission and distribution networks. Our engineering staff is highly experienced and receive regular specialist training. The company management is committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace that will protect the wellbeing of employees and clients alike.

Today, we offer the full range of skills in Substation construction (Electrical), Testing Commissioning and Maintenance field. We provide the active Leadership and technical support to achieve high quality standards of Engineering and Management. The organization provides the services of construction, testing & commissioning and Maintenance in Medium Voltage switchgears, Transformers, HV Cable, Protection and Control Panels in 380kV, 220kV, 132kV, 33kV, 13.8kV, 4.16kV and 400V Indoor, Outdoor & GIS Substations.

We have a wide range of expertise in site electrical infrastructure and Sub-Station connection in renewables, data centres, manufacturing and pharmaceuticals in Ireland, Netherlands and the UK. With our understanding of Grid codes and HV interface requirements we are able to guide our clients through the grid connection process.

Provide Energy Efficient and Cost Effective Solution to Power, Process & Product Industries in order to meet their global challenges on time & every time and having flexibility to upgrade for future needs. Offer the best solutions to customers. Exceed the expectation of customer is ours focus.
